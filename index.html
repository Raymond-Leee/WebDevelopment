Two-Layer Tic Tac Toe
  • Click on an empty space to claim it.
  • Three in a row wins!
  • Click on a space again to permanently lock it in! No one else can get it once it's locked in! (Marked in Red)
  • Click on your opponent's space in order to take their spot from them!
  • You can't immediately take your opponent's spot! You'll have to wait a turn first!
